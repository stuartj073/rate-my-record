What is Rate my Record?

What it is

Rate my Record aims to bring together music lovers through providing an online platform in which user's can contribute with their own personal review's of all things music. A user can choose to either rate a Record or a store of their own choosing, provide some basic information such as Album name or store name, and then give their own take on the record or store in question through their description of it. The aim of the site is to marry those looking at gaining new musical knowledge, be it in the form of some online digging or just acquiring the music in it's physical form through buying a record online or even visiting a record store itself.


Sounds good? Sign up right here!

What it isn't

This isn't a platform to blatantly slate an artist, album or record store. It is just to voice your own opinion on whatever you decide to make a review about. Constructive feedback is encouraged but there is a certain threshold that is crossed where an opinion can verge into downright criticism. That is not what This website is about. It is to inform and educate those wishing to learn and acquire knowledge on whatever it might be that they're looking for. Any posts deemed un-friendly, too critical or that contain inappropriate language will be removed as will the user's associated with these posts. If you come across such a post please don't hesitate to contact us here and we shall look to rectify the issue promptly and swiftly.
